MEDICAL LEAVE

It is typically granted for health-related reasons, including illness, injury, surgery, or the management of chronic health conditions. Students require documentation to support the need for medical leave. This may include a doctor's note or medical certificate detailing the nature of the illness or condition and the recommended duration of leave.It can be applied only the attendence is above 75%.

EXTRA-CURRICULAR LEAVE

Student is granted leave from their regular academic activities to participate in extracurricular activities, events, competitions etc. It is to encourage students to engage in activities outside the regular academic curriculum, such as sports, cultural events, etc. It recognizes the value of holistic development beyond classroom learning. It can be applied only the attendence is above 75%.

PLACEMENT LEAVE

It is typically granted for those students who are attending placement talks, interviews, group dicussions ,etc related to placements which is organised by the university and this is only applicable for third year students of UG and master courses.Before applying for this leave it should be informed to placement representative It can be applied only the attendence is above 75%.
